Season 5 - Episode 1: "In Order to Defeat Muzan Kibutsuji!"

The Hashira hold a meeting at Ubuyashiki Mansion while Tanjiro recovers. Later, they devise a training plan for the entire Demon Slayer Corps. The demon's head falls to the floor while his immense body begins to crumble to ash, angered that the last thing he will see is the face of a Demon Slayer. Tanjiro prays for the Hand Demon. In the afterlife, the demon remembers his regret of devouring his brother soon after transforming. Two Upper Rank demons were defeated in the Swordsmith Village, thanks to Tanjiro, his comrades, and two Hashira's efforts and bravery. However, the fact that Nezuko has conquered the sun foreshadows the imminence of a large-scale battle against Muzan Kibutsuji.
